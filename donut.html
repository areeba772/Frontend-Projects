Our Story

Baked with love
since 2009

What started as a tiny stall in Lahore's Liberty Market has grown into Pakistan's most loved artisan donut brand. But the recipe — and the love — has never changed.

🍩
The Beginning

A sweet idea that became a movement

In 2009, Chef Fatima Mirza opened a tiny kiosk in Liberty Market with nothing but a borrowed fryer, her grandmother's dough recipe, and a dream. She sold 40 donuts on day one — by week two, she had a queue around the block.

Today, Glazed & Dazed operates 3 locations across Lahore and delivers to Karachi and Islamabad. Every single donut is still handmade using the same original dough recipe — just scaled up with a lot more love.
